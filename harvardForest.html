Harvard Forest Schoolyard Ecology Website Redesign

A redesigned website for the Harvard Forest (link) Schoolyard Ecology Resources page.

Problem

Currently, the Harvard Forest Schoolyard Ecology page often visited by educators that partner with Harvard Forest and Utilize their projects find the resources page extremely difficult to use as it is pages of links to the resources with minimal descriptions.

Objective

The objective of this project was thus to redesign the resources page to allow better accessibility and usability, keeping in mind user needs and requirements.
