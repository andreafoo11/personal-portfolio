In Our Own Words

A project that seeks to harness the power of storytelling to celebrate and bring awareness to history, experiences, and diversity within the AAPI community.

Overview

  • Company:

    Harvard University, funded by the CLIF Grant

  • Timeline:

    September – December 2022

Skills

  • Technical:

    Canva, Figma, Wix

  • Soft:

    Communication, remote collaboration, project management, timeline management, project planning

Context

Stories from AAPI community members at Harvard will be collected, archived, and compiled into a virtual platform that will also feature Harvard’s records and materials about AAPI history and culture. This project will culminate in a live storytelling event to heal and rebuild the community in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Takeaways

As the UIUX designer for the website for this project, my job was to compile content and generate ways to present the content for both the mobile and web version. I used Figma to prototype ideas and Canva to create graphics I can put on the website. This project was meaningful to me because as a member of the AAPI community, I find it so important to illuminate the voices of the AAPI community as we are often “forgotten”. Through this project, my team and I hoped that we would be able to tell the stories of people in the AAPI community at Harvard and to make sure that all their stories were heard.
